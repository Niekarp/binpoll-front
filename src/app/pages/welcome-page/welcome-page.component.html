Welcome headset
This website contains a listening test which takes about 10 minutes to complete. During the test you will be asked to listen to {{ testCount }} short music recordings and to indicate the direction where an ensemble of musicians is located. It requires using headphones.

It’s part of the research into perception of 3D audio carried out at Bialystok University of Technology and Huddersfield University. The tests are coordinated by Dr S. Zielinski (email: s.zielinski@pb.edu.pl).

For just the research purposes, we gather anonymous data, including information regarding listeners’ age, background, and headphones. We also use cookies for session management. By continuing, you agree to our terms and cookie policy.
